Jameson Stafford – Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Jameson, his wife Shanna and their beloved Rhodesian ridgebacks Murphy and Stella moved to Incline Village full-time in 2015. Jameson is passionate about technology, whether it’s consumer applications, SaaS platforms, or digital filmmaking. He has been fortunate enough to spend his days following this passion and has built a diverse career around it. He has founded multiple businesses, including several tech startups, two of which were acquired by larger public companies. He has grown and managed US and international teams in excess of 50 employees both pre and post-acquisition. As a full-stack developer, his cloud-based platforms have supported millions of users, creating user-generated content through a content publishing platform that he created from the ground-up. In his spare time, he enjoys making films and music videos and has had the pleasure of directing talented actors and musicians, ranging from Sean Penn, Kid Rock, Jada Pinkett-Smith and Erykah Badu. Most recently, Jameson has turned his energy and passion towards a new venture, Elevate Blue, a startup studio on the shores of Lake Tahoe. Here, Jameson has brought an ambitious team of developers, executives, investors and young entrepreneurs together to build technology-based businesses and drive economic growth for the Northern Nevada region.

Dan Eckert – Chief Development Officer & Co-Founder

Dan lives in Incline Village year round with his wife and two sons. Dan was an inaugural investor in Elevate Blue and joined the team as Chief Development Officer in June of 2016. Dan has over 30 years of experience in technology enabled services, specialized distribution and logistics and medical devices. He has held multiple senior executive positions and served on the board of directors of several private venture stage, private equity backed and publicly traded companies. He was a member of the leadership team that took Neoforma (NEOF) public in a $100 million IPO in 2000, has successfully raised early stage capital from top tier VCs for 2 start-up medical device/software companies and participated in multiple successful mid-cap private equity build outs and exits. Dan specializes in strategy, corporate alliances, fundraising, new market and product development, sourcing and building leadership teams, sales, operational execution, M&A and business/cultural integration. Dan has a passion for education and leadership development. He served on the board of trustees of Verde Valley School, an international school in Sedona, Arizona and he serves as the executive sponsor of Code Tahoe.

Scott Levy – Chief Business Officer & Co-Founder

Scott is a passionate business leader and mentor. In roles as CEO, Co-founder, CMO and GM he has been engaged in turn arounds, M&A, start ups, branding, successful exits and building Global Technology Strategic Alliances. Since moving to Incline Village in 2014 with his wife and young son, Scott has become highly involved in the community. As an active member of the Sierra Angels, he sits on their deal Screening Committee. He is also very involved with Sierra Nevada College's entrepreneurship program, pitch and business plan competitions. In addition to being an avid skier, outdoorsman and family man, Scott is very involved with the International leadership organization, YPO, and sits on the board of YPO Gold NorCal.

Tim Olesnavage – Co-Founder and General Manager of Code Tahoe

Tim is originally from Grand Rapids, MI and has spent the last 10 years working in the New York, SF, and LA tech scenes, and has finally come to his senses. Kelly Benson – KickParty Product Manager
